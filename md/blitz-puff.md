# Blitz Puff Pastry

## Ingredients
 - 1½ cup flour
 - 12 tbsp butter in ½" cubes
 - 1 tsp salt
 - ½ cup water, cold

## Instructions

Combine and form. Chill 30 minutes. Letter book book. Chill 10 mins. Book. Chill 30 minutes.

Bake at 400°F for 22 minutes (note: probably longer).
