# Cinnamon Roll

## Ingredients
 - ½ cup milk, warm (110°F)
 - 2 tsp yeast
 - 2½ cup flour, all purpose
 - ½ tsp salt
 - ¼ cup sugar
 - ¼ cup margarine, melted
 - 2 eggs

### Filling
 - Butter, cut into small cubes
 - Brown sugar
 - Cinnamon

### Icing
 - Powdered sugar
 - Vanilla extract
 - Water

## Instructions

 - Make dough. Knead. Rise 1 hour. Roll out. Add filling while forming. Rise ½ hour. Bake at 400°F for 15 minutes.
