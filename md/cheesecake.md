# Cheesecake

## Ingredients
 - 2 lb (4 six oz. packages) cream cheese
 - 1/2 cup sour cream
 - 1 cup sugar
 - 3 egg yolks
 - 1 whole egg
 - 1/8 tsp salt
 - 1 tsp vanilla extract
 - 2 tsp lemon juice

 - 12 graham crackers
 - 5 tbsp butter

## Instructions


### Graham cracker crust
 1. Wrap 9" springform pan in aluminum foil to make water-tight seal.
 2. Crush graham crackers. Melt butter. Combine.
 3. Transfer mixture to pan and press with glass.
 4. Bake at 350Â°F until starting to brown, 8-10 minutes.
