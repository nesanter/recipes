# Popovers

## Ingredients
 - 1 cup flour
 - ½ tsp salt
 - 1 cup milk
 - 2 eggs

## Instructions

 1. Preheat oven to 450°. Grease muffin tin.
 2. Briefly heat the muffin tin in the oven.
 3. Whisk together ingredients. Ladle into tin (about 9 spots.)
 4. Bake for 20 minutes. Reduce heat to 350°. Bake for 20 minutes.
