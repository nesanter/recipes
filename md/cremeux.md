# Chocolate crémeux

## Ingredients
 - 105 g whole milk
 - 105 g heavy cream
 - 2 egg yolks
 - 30 g sugar
 - 130 g chocolate

## Instructions

 1. Break chocolate into container. Set aside.
 2. Combine milk, cream. Heat.
 3. Beat yolks and sugar.
 4. Temper, heat.
 5. Pour over chocolate, stir to combine.
 6. Return to heat. Cook until done.
