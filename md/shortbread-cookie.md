# Shortbread Cookie

Makes 6-8 cookies.

## Ingredients
 - 1 stick butter
 - ½ cup sugar
 - 1 cup flour

## Instructions

 1. Combine butter and sugar, mixing thoroughly with pastry cutter.
 2. Add flour slowly, about ¼ cup at a time, mixing between until dough is correct.
 3. Bake for 20-25 minutes at 350°F.
