# Buffalo Tots

## Ingredients
 - Small bottle of Frank's RedHot
 - Half a stick of butter
 - splash or two of worchestershire

 - Bag of tots
 - Bag of 4 cheese mex
 - Wedge of original blue
 - garlic powder

 - some cilantro
 - squiz of ranch
